Hello! This is the main landing page for a website about golden retrievers.

On this website you can learn about the origin and history of the golden retriever dog breed. The website describes how some golden retrievers were used as service dogs, along with the general personality traits of the breed. The website mentions some important supplies to have as a golden retriever owner, and the potential health risks golden retrievers are prone to have. Lastly, there is information on the living conditions golden retrievers are usually suited for, and how much daily exercise they need. Please use the table of contents to go to each page.
