Traits and Accomplishments

The golden retriever is known for its obedience and intelligence, which makes it ideal as a service dog. Golden retrievers can be trained to be guide dogs, therapy dogs, and even search dogs. One such example was the Bretagne, a Golden Retriever search dog that had helped search for survivors during 9/11. Bretagne also continued as a service dog during Hurricane Rita and Katrina. To learn more about Bretagne, visit this website.

Retrievers in Movies

Besides being service dogs, golden retrievers have become popular in pop culture. The family movie "Air Bud" features Buddy, a golden retriever that can play sports, who helps high schooler Josh with basketball. "Air Buddies" also consisted of five golden retriever puppies that go on miscellaneous adventures.

  • "Air Buddies" includes five of Tucker's puppies:
  • B-Dawg
    • The "cool" dog known for having a feisty personality, although he is secretly easily scared. He is the second-oldest puppy, and wears a silver chain necklace in the films.
  • MudBud
    • MudBud likes to roll in the dirt. He is the youngest boy of the litter.
  • Rosebud
    • The only female puppy in the litter, Rosebud wears a pink bow on her head. Although she is personified in the films as being easily annoyed at her brothers' antics, she is also shown to be very protective of them.

Budderball is a bit of a glutton, and his love for food can often distract him and gets into trouble.

Personality

Golden Retrievers generally have a fair temperment, which can make them suitable for children and the rest of the family. However, given their size, golden retrievers are not always suitable for extremely small children (although this depends on the dog and family situation). Golden retrievers are not especially known for barking, and are generally not watchdogs because they typically lack strict guarding instincts. They also tend to be active dogs, requiring ample amounts of time for exercise. For more information about the personality of golden retrievers, use this website.
