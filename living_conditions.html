Living Conditions

Golden retrievers are fairly big dogs. They usually need 20 to 30 minutes of exercise twice a day, depending on how active or young the dog is. Golden retrievers are known for being heavy shedders. They shed moderately during the summer and winter, but shed more extensively in the spring and fall.

Swimming

Since golden retrievers were originally bred to retrieve hunting game from lakes or rivers, golden retrievers are typically swimmers. While not always necessary, golden retrievers typically enjoy swimming. To learn more about the love of swimming golden retrievers have, please see this website.
